Returns & Exchanges

1. Our Philosophy

At EVORAA CLOTHING, we strive for visual excellence and premium comfort. If a garment does not meet your expectations in sizing or visual feel, we are happy to facilitate an exchange or refund under the guidelines below.

2. Time Frame for Returns

Item returns and exchanges must be requested within **7 calendar days** of the delivery date. Items received beyond this window are eligible for store credit at the discretion of management.

3. Eligible Garment Condition

All returned items must be unworn, unwashed, unaltered, and completely free of cosmetic marks, perfumes, or deodorants. Items must retain their original tags, label seals, and designer packaging.

4. Process for Exchange

To initiate an exchange, please email our support at **contact@evoraa.lk** with your Order ID and size preference. Courier collection for Colombo orders will be coordinated by our logistics partners. For islandwide orders, customers must ship the item back to our Rosmead Place showroom.
